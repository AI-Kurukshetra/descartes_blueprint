## 5-MINUTE VIDEO SCRIPT TEMPLATE

> Fill in [BRACKETS] when you have your project definition.
> Practice once. Record once. Authentic beats scripted.

---

### 0:00 – 0:30 | THE HOOK

**Say this (or close to it):**

"[TARGET USER] wastes [X time / X money] every [week/month] on [PROBLEM].
Existing tools like [COMPETITOR] are either too expensive, too complicated, 
or just not built for how [Indian teams / small businesses / freelancers] work.
So I built [PRODUCT NAME]. And I built it in 10 hours."

> Tip: Be on camera for this part. Eye contact builds trust.

---

### 0:30 – 2:30 | THE DEMO

**Show in this order:**
1. Open the live app URL in browser
2. Login with demo@[app].com (show it's instant, smooth)
3. Walk the dashboard — point out the stats, say what they mean
4. Do the primary user flow end-to-end:
   - "A [user type] would come here, see this..."
   - "They'd click [action]..."
   - "Fill this in..."
   - "And in seconds — [result]"
5. Show Feature 2 briefly
6. Show mobile view (use Chrome DevTools responsive mode or phone)

> Say less, show more. Let the product talk.

---

### 2:30 – 3:30 | TECH STACK

**Say:**
"This is built on Next.js 14 with App Router, Supabase for the database 
and auth, deployed on Vercel. I used Claude CLI to build almost all of this — 
the AI handled scaffolding, database schema, components, and debugging. 
I directed it like a product manager and it coded like a senior engineer."

> This positions you well — not "I cheated with AI" but "I used AI strategically."

---

### 3:30 – 4:30 | WHY BETTER

**Focus on 2-3 sharp points:**
- "Unlike [COMPETITOR], this is [specific advantage — simpler / cheaper / India-focused / faster]"
- "It takes [30 seconds / 2 clicks] to [primary action] vs [competitor's complex flow]"
- "[SPECIFIC FEATURE] that [COMPETITOR] doesn't have"

> Confidence here. You built something real.

---

### 4:30 – 5:00 | CTA

**End with the Bacancy CTA + your personal note:**

"[PRODUCT NAME] is live right now at [URL]. Try it — the demo account is already loaded.
And if you want to build and launch products like this — fast, clean, and production-ready —
Bacancy is the team that makes it happen.
Build fast. Scale smart. Stay secure. Connect with Bacancy."

## 🚀 PRODUCT HUNT LAUNCH KIT

> Fill in [BRACKETS] with your actual product details.
> Pre-write ALL of this before hackathon day.

---

### TAGLINE (60 chars max — make it punchy)
```
[Primary benefit] for [target user] — without [pain point]
```

Examples of good taglines:
- "Async standups that don't kill your flow"
- "Restaurant menus that sell, not just list"
- "Client proposals that close, not bore"

---

### SHORT DESCRIPTION (260 chars max)

```
[PRODUCT NAME] helps [TARGET USER] [DO PRIMARY ACTION] in [TIME/CLICKS].

Unlike [COMPETITOR], it's [KEY DIFFERENTIATOR]. Built for [SPECIFIC CONTEXT].

Try the live demo — no signup needed to explore.
```

---

### SCREENSHOTS PLAN (minimum 3)

1. **Hero shot** — Dashboard home with full seed data, stats, charts
2. **Core feature** — The primary feature page in action
3. **Mobile view** — Shows you thought about mobile
4. **Empty → filled** — Before/after of creating something (optional)

> Take screenshots on the live Vercel URL with seed data populated.
> Use a 1440px wide browser window for desktop shots.
