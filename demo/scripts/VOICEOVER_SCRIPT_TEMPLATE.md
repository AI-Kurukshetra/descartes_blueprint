## Recording Tips

1. **Pace:** Read at a moderate pace, ~150 words per minute
2. **Tone:** Professional but approachable
3. **Emphasis:** Highlight key features like "AI-powered", "automated", "real-time"
4. **Pauses:** Brief pauses between sections to let visuals breathe
5. **Energy:** Keep energy consistent throughout

## Text-to-Speech Options

If generating AI voiceover:
- **ElevenLabs:** Best quality, use "Adam" or "Rachel" voice
- **Play.ht:** Good alternative
- **AWS Polly:** Budget option, use "Matthew" neural voice
